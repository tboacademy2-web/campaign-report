CAMPAIGN CONTEXT

Connecting Canadian travel professionals with Washington, DC

Washington, D.C. is a global centre of history, politics and culture. The campaign equipped travel professionals with destination knowledge, itinerary ideas, hotel options and bookable experiences. TBO Academy supported the activity through educational content, platform visibility, direct marketing and measurable engagement across the travel-agent journey.

Management insight
  • The campaign combined awareness, education and booking-oriented content instead of relying on a single communication channel.
  • The portrait report format keeps evidence, numbers and recommendations consistent across clients.
